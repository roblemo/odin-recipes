How to make PBNJ

The classic Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich is a quick, easy, and nostalgic favorite enjoyed by all ages. With creamy or crunchy peanut butter and your favorite jam or jelly spread between two slices of bread, it’s the perfect balance of sweet and salty.

This simple sandwich is ideal for a fast lunch, snack, or even a picnic. It’s endlessly customizable — use any type of bread, nut butter, or fruit spread to make it your own!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, wheat, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
  • 2 tablespoons of jelly or jam (grape, strawberry, or preferred flavor)
  • Butter or margarine (optional, for toasting)

Steps

  1. Lay out both slices of bread on a clean surface or plate.
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread jelly or jam evenly on the other slice.
  4. Press the two slices together, peanut butter and jelly sides facing each other.
  5. Optional: Lightly butter the outsides and toast the sandwich in a skillet for 1–2 minutes per side until golden brown.
  6. Slice in half and serve immediately. Enjoy!
