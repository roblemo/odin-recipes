Recipe Title Lasagna Recipe

How to make the best Lasagna!

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, melted mozzarella, and savory Parmesan. Each bite combines tender noodles, flavorful tomato sauce, and perfectly baked cheese for a hearty, satisfying meal.

This dish is perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or meal prep — it tastes even better the next day. Whether you make it with beef, sausage, or vegetables, lasagna brings warmth and tradition to any table.

Items

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Olive oil
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato paste
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned; drain excess fat
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, herbs, salt, and pepper; simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and a bit of Parmesan until smooth
  7. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a baking dish
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, sauce, and mozzarella cheese; repeat until all ingredients are used
  9. Top with a final layer of sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes; uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  11. Let rest for 15 minutes before serving
